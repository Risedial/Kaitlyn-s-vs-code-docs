### DISCARDED

**sIgA — SHP → Calprotectin/Lactoferrin**
- Reason: Calprotectin and lactoferrin are innate inflammatory markers activated by neutrophil recruitment; sIgA SHP depletion is an adaptive immune marker. These operate in mechanistically distinct compartments — sIgA insufficiency does not directly drive calprotectin/lactoferrin elevation, and their co-occurrence reflects shared upstream inflammation rather than a direct structural connection. Insufficient independent evidence for a validated link.

**sIgA — SHP → Occult Blood**
- Reason: Occult blood reflects mucosal hemorrhage from mechanical or inflammatory erosion; sIgA insufficiency does not directly cause mucosal hemorrhage. Co-occurrence in advanced GI pathology represents common upstream cause (severe inflammation/dysbiosis) rather than a direct structural connection between the two markers. No independent evidence for a direct link.
