### Pregnenolone Steal Clarification
Pregnenolone Steal and Steroidogenesis Disruption is a downstream mechanism, not a direct lab value. Its variable card must reflect this distinction explicitly. The card should read: "Downstream mechanism (not a direct lab value) — cortisol demand diverts pregnenolone away from DHEA and sex hormone synthesis." It is flagged isCrossPanel: true.

### Blood Glucose Dysregulation Note
Blood Glucose Dysregulation appears as a variable reference in several symptoms (Afternoon energy crash, Wake up between 1–3am, Irritability / short fuse, Mood swings) but is not included in the Variable Reference Panel as a standalone variable card. It functions as a contextual clinical note within those symptoms rather than a panel-measured marker.

### MRT Reactive Foods Note
